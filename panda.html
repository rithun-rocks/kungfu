Po, The Dragon Warrior

About Po

Po aka The Dragon Warrior is a character from a famous animated movie, "Kung-Fu Panda". It's first movie was released on 2008, then they released 2 more of its series on 2011 and 2016 respectively and was rated PG. The character is a fat, fluffy panda who likes 2 things-eating and fighting. He got selected as the dragon master by The Grand Master Oogway and then got training by Master Shifu and The Furious Five. It is a really nice movie and you gotta see it!
